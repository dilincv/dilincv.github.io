About Prof. Di Lin

Di Lin

Doctoral Advisor

Di Lin is a tenured associate professor with the College of Intelligence and Computing, Tianjin University. He received a Ph.D. degree from the Computer Science and Engineering Department at the Chinese University of Hong Kong and a B.Eng. degree in software engineering at Sun Yat-Sen University, China. His research interests include computer vision and machine learning. He has been recognized as one of the Stanford/Elsevier World's Top 2% Scientists from 2022 to now.
